Market Announcement
6 July 2020

Blossomvale Holdings Limited (ASX: BLV) – Suspension from
Official Quotation
Description
The securities of Blossomvale Holdings Limited (‘BLV’) will be suspended from quotation immediately under
Listing Rule 17.2, following release of the results of its general meeting, where shareholders approved the
resolution to delist from ASX Limited (‘ASX’).
BLV will remain suspended until it is removed from the Official List of ASX in accordance with Listing Rule 17.11.

Issued by
Simon Barcham
Listings Compliance (Perth)
