Aurora Labs Limited (ASX: A3D) – Suspension from Official
Quotation
Description
The securities of Aurora Labs Limited (‘A3D’) will be suspended from quotation immediately under Listing Rule
17.3, to enable A3D to respond to an ASX Price and Volume Query. The securities will remain suspended until
A3D has provided a response to the ASX Price and Volume Query.
