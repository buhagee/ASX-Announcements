Trading Halt Request
Xped Limited (ASX: XPE) (“Xped” or “the Company”) requests an immediate
trading halt be granted by the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) for all of its
securities.
In accordance with Listing Rule 17.1 Xped advises:
1.

The trading halt is requested pending an announcement of a settlement
agreement.

2.

The trading halt is requested until the earlier of the commencement of
normal trading on Thursday, 9 July 2020 or when the announcement is
released to the market.

3.

The Company is not aware of any reason why trading in its securities
should not be temporarily halted.
