Estrella Resources Limited – Request for Trading Halt
In accordance with Listing Rule 17.1, Estrella Resources Limited (“Estrella Resources” or “the
Company”) hereby requests a trading halt from ASX effective immediately, pending an
announcement regarding a potential asset disposal.

The Company requests the trading halt last from receipt of the request until the release of the
announcement, in any case no later than the commencement of trading on Thursday, 9 July
2020.

The Company is not aware of any reason why the trading halt should not be granted, nor any
other information necessary to inform the market about the trading halt.
