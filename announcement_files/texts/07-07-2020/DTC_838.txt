Damstra Holdings Limited (ASX: DTC) – Trading Halt
Description
The securities of Damstra Holdings Limited (‘DTC’) will be placed in trading halt at the request of DTC, pending
it releasing an announcement. Unless ASX decides otherwise, the securities will remain in trading halt until the
earlier of the commencement of normal trading on Thursday, 9 July 2020 or when the announcement is
released to the market.
