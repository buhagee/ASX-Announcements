Ora Banda Mining Ltd (ASX: OBM) – Trading Halt
Description
The securities of Ora Banda Mining Ltd (‘OBM’) will be placed in trading halt at the request of OBM, pending it
releasing an announcement. Unless ASX decides otherwise, the securities will remain in trading halt until the
earlier of the commencement of normal trading on Tuesday, 7 July 2020 or when the announcement is released
to the market.

Request for Trading Halt
Pursuant to ASX Listing Rule 17.1, the Directors of Ora Banda Mining Ltd (“Ora Banda” or the “Company”)
requests that an immediate trading halt be placed on the securities of the Company pending release of an
announcement to the ASX regarding a capital raising.
Ora Banda anticipates that the trading halt will be lifted on or before market open on Tuesday 7 July 2020.
The Company is not aware of any reason why the trading halt should not be granted.
