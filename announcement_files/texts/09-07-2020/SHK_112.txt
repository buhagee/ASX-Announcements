JUNE	  QUARTER	  ACTIVITY	  REPORT	  
	  
1.	   Overview	  
	  

A	  summary	  on	  the	  main	  items	  of	  activities	  for	  Stone	  Resources	  Australia	  Limited	  
(“the	  Company”)	  is	  outlined	  below:	  
•

The	  Company	  has	  entered	  into	  a	  deed	  of	  variation	  that	  extends	  the	  term	  of	  the	  
Debt	  and	  Equity	  Compromise	  Agreement	  with	  its	  major	  debt	  provider	  and	  
major	  shareholder.	  
Please	  see	  ASX	  announcement	  dated	  29	  June	  2020	  for	  details.	  

	  
•

The	  Company	  continues	  to	  investigate	  suitable	  opportunities	  for	  the	  development	  of	  
the	  Ben	  Hur,	  Alpha,	  Corktree	  Well	  and	  Beta	  projects.	  	  

	  
2.	   Corporate	  
	  
The	  Company	  received	  continuous	  funding	  from	  the	  parent	  entity,	  Stone	  Resources	  Limited,	  
for	  exploration	  and	  working	  capital	  requirements.	  During	  the	  quarter	  the	  Company	  received	  
approximately	  $92,000	  from	  the	  parent	  entity.	  
	  
	  
3.	   Tenement	  Schedule

refer	  to	  the	  addendum	  to	  this	  report.
