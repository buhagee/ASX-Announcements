Estrella Resources Limited – Request for Voluntary Suspension
In accordance with Listing Rule 17.2, Estrella Resources Limited (“Estrella Resources” or “the
Company”) hereby requests a voluntary suspension of the Company’s securities pending the
announcement regarding a potential asset disposal.

An announcement will be made to the market regarding the potential asset disposal which is
expected to end the period of voluntary suspension and will be made on or before Monday,
13 July 2020.

The Company is not aware of any reason why the voluntary suspension should not be granted,
nor any other information necessary to inform the market about the voluntary suspension.
