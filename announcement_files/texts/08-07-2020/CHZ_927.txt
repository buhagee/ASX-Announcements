Chesser Resources Limited – Request for Trading Halt
Chesser Resources Limited (ASX:CHZ) requests that an immediate voluntary trading halt be granted by the
ASX with respect to its securities.
In accordance with ASX Listing Rule 17.1, Chesser provides the following information:
1. The trading halt is requested by Chesser pending an announcement in relation to a proposed capital
raising.
2. Chesser requests that the trading halt remain in place until the earlier of an announcement to the
market in relation to the capital raising, or the commencement of trading on Friday 10 July 2020.
3. Chesser is not aware of any reason why the trading halt should not be granted.

This request for a trading halt is authorised by the Board of Directors of Chesser Resources Limited

ABOUT CHESSER RESOURCES
Chesser Resources is an ASX listed exploration company with gold projects located in Senegal, West Africa.
The Company’s focus is its extensive landholding of gold projects within Senegal’s most prospective gold belts.
The Company has a corporate office located in Brisbane, Australia and a corporate and technical team based
in Dakar, Senegal.
