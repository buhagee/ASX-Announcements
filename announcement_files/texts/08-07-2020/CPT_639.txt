COMPLETION OF PLACEMENT AND APPENDIX 2A

Cipherpoint Limited (ASX:CPT), Sydney, Australia Wednesday 8 July 2020 – Cipherpoint
Limited (ASX:CPT) is pleased to confirm that the ordinary shares subscribed under the
placement announced yesterday have been allotted.
A total of 19,000,000 ordinary shares were allotted raising $437,000. An Appendix 2A
accompanies this announcement.
This announcement has been authorised by the Board.

multiple platforms and repositories, including industry best security for Microsoft SharePoint.
Our customers and partners span the public sector, defence, utilities, health, logistics, finance and
education.
