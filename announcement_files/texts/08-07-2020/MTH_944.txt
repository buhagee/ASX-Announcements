The Company proposes to undertake a total placement of 194,444,444 shares at $0.018 per share. Forming part of the

Silver project has also committed to 22,000,000 shares. Related and/or associated party participation in the placement
(45,000,000 shares) will be subject to shareholder approval at a meeting that will be held in August 2020.
