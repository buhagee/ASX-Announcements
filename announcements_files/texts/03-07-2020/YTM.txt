Market Announcement
3 July 2020

Equity Trustees Limited – Suspension of XTB EQT TLS 7.75%
JUL-20 (ASX Code: YTMTLS) from Trading Status
Description
The securities XTB EQT TLS 7.75% JUL-20 (ASX Code: YTMTLS) (the ‘Units’) of Equity Trustees Limited (the ‘Issuer’)
will be suspended at the close of trading today on Friday, 3 July 2020, in accordance with the announcement
lodged by the Issuer dated 21 January 2020, which advised of the final coupon payment and repayment of the
principal of the underlying bond on 20 July 2020.
Please note that the suspension only applies to the Units (ASX Code: YTMTLS), and does not apply to any
other quoted securities of the Issuer.
