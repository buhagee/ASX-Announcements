Red Metal Limited (ASX: RDM) – Trading Halt
Description
The securities of Red Metal Limited (‘RDM’) will be placed in trading halt at the request of RDM, pending it
releasing an announcement. Unless ASX decides otherwise, the securities will remain in trading halt until the
earlier of the commencement of normal trading on Friday, 10 July 2020 or when the announcement is released
to the market.

TRADING HALT
Red Metal Limited (ASX: RDM, “the Company”) requests that a trading halt of its securities be
granted by ASX with effect from prior to commencement of trading today Wednesday, 8 July 2020.
For the purposes of Listing Rule 17.1, the Company provides the following information:
(a) The trading halt is requested for the purposes of finalising a capital raising.
(b) The Company requests the trading halt remains in place until the commencement of trading
on Friday, 10 July 2020 unless before that time the Company makes an announcement in
relation to the capital raising.
(c) The Company is not aware of any reason why the trading halt should not be granted.
This ASX announcement was authorised by Mr Rob Rutherford, Managing Director of Red Metal
Limited.
