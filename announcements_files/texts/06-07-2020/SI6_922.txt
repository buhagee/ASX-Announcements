Six Sigma Metals Limited (ASX: SI6) – Trading Halt (2+2)
Description
The securities of Six Sigma Metals Limited (‘SI6’) will be placed in trading halt at the request of SI6, pending it
releasing an announcement regarding a capital raising. Unless ASX decides otherwise, the securities will remain
in trading halt until the earlier of the commencement of normal trading on Friday, 10 July 2020 or when the
announcement is released to the market.

Request for Trading Halt
Six Sigma Metals Limited (ASX:SI6) (“Six Sigma” or “the Company”) requests that the
securities of the Company be placed in a trading halt as follows:

2.

be granted.
