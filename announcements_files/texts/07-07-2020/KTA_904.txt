Krakatoa Resources Limited (ASX: KTA) – Trading Halt (2+2)
Description
The securities of Krakatoa Resources Limited (‘KTA’) will be placed in trading halt at the request of KTA,
pending it releasing an announcement regarding a capital raising. Unless ASX decides otherwise, the securities
will remain in trading halt until the earlier of the commencement of normal trading on Monday, 13 July 2020 or
when the announcement is released to the market.

TRADING HALT REQUEST
